The Architecture

System Component 01: The Occupancy Engine

An institutional grade sales infrastructure that converts interest into occupancy without human intervention.

Current State: The Leaky Bucket

The typical care home process: generic email inbox checked when spare moments allow, often 4 to 24 hours later. Manual phone call follow up with one or two attempts, then the lead is abandoned. Losing one private pay resident due to a slow response costs the home £75,000 to £100,000 in annual lost fees.

From a digital black hole to a high speed pipeline.

We don't sell AI chatbots. We install instant response infrastructure. When a family reaches out, silence is replaced by an empathy driven, professional capture system that secures the Speed to Trust.

  • Instant Capture: Systems monitor your website and Carehome.co.uk enquiries in real-time, 24/7.
  • 30 Second AI Voice Call: Within 30 seconds of an enquiry, a professional call is initiated to the family to validate needs and gather essential details.
  • Elite Qualification: Strategic questioning regarding budget, timeline, and urgency to identify high intent private pay leads instantly.
  • Calendar Integration: Synchronizes with your team's real-time availability to secure tours directly into your master calendar.
  • Multi Channel Nurture: Automatic text and email follow ups with digital brochures and testimonials keep the family warm for 14 days.
  • GHL CRM Sync: Full transcriptions of every conversation are logged so the manager walks into the tour knowing exactly what the family needs.

System Component 02: The Recruitment Engine

An aggressive, automated direct hire infrastructure designed to reduce agency dependency and stabilize your workforce.

Current State: The Vacancy Crisis

Standard Indeed ad placement with passive waiting for CVs. CVs sit in inboxes for 3 to 5 days while managers cover clinical shifts. Good candidates are hired by faster competitors before you call. DBS and Right to Work checks take weeks of manual email tag. Carer shelf life on Indeed is under 24 hours. Agency markups cost £150 to £300 extra per day per vacant role.

Stop paying the 40% Impatience Tax to agencies.

By engaging every applicant within 60 seconds, we build your private staff bank, ensuring you secure the best talent before an agency even sees the application.

  • The Indeed Magnet: Real time sync with Indeed and LinkedIn. The second a carer applies, the engine intercepts the data instantly.
  • Instant Candidate Engagement: Voice engine contacts every applicant within 2 minutes of their submission.
  • Compliance Triage: Confirms mandatory deal breakers including certification levels, right to work, and shift availability before a manager reviews a CV.
  • Candidate Scoring: Every screening call is scored against role requirements automatically.
  • Manager Dashboard: Every applicant, their score, and their full screening transcript in one place.
  • Direct Hire Velocity: Operating in seconds rather than days, you secure staff for the cost of an ad rather than a £5,000 agency fee.

System Component 03: The Staff Oracle

A private, automated support layer that protects your workforce stability and prevents the manager level bottleneck.

Current State: The Support Vacuum

200 page paper handbooks provided at induction but never referenced again. Carers leave the floor to find a manager for routine policy questions, wasting 20 minutes each time. Stressed staff don't complain — they ghost or resign when it's already too late. Losing a single experienced carer costs £6,000. For a mid sized home, turnover at 30% creates a silent £90,000 annual loss.

Protect your managers from administrative saturation.

Replace manual policy lookups with systemic intelligence. The Oracle provides your workforce with instant, compliant answers to routine questions.

  • Digital Knowledge Core: Your 200 page handbook, CQC policies and SOPs are indexed into a high precision database for instant, compliant recall.
  • WhatsApp Oracle Interface: Carers text the system directly for help with reporting, maintenance or procedures without leaving the floor.
  • 3 Second Compliant Support: Immediate, policy backed guidance for any staff query, ensuring every action is compliant with your latest SOPs and safety protocols.
  • Manager Escalation Logic: If the Oracle doesn't know, it pings the manager directly to update the core, turning one question into a system wide upgrade.
  • Moral Sentiment Tracking: The system identifies clusters of questions around topics like payroll or exhaustion and alerts owners to potential morale issues.
  • Retention Infrastructure: Eliminates the First 90 Day Friction so staff feel supported and capable from day one.

Built for Stability

Bespoke Tailoring: We don't use templates. We map your home's unique pricing, room availability, and care philosophy into the system's logic core.
