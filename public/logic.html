Your Installation Roadmap: 8 Days to Live

A structured, five week process from operational audit to live infrastructure. Every step is documented, tested, and approved by your team.

  • Day 1 — Understanding: We conduct a deep dive into your home's current enquiry volume, recruitment needs, and staff workflow. Deliverable: operational audit report with bottleneck analysis.
  • Days 2 to 5 — Installation: Our engineers build your custom growth infrastructure. No complex IT integration is required. Deliverable: voice systems, CRM workflows, and handbook assistant configured.
  • Days 5 to 6 — Tailoring: The system is refined with your specific inputs — room types, pricing models, and home culture. Deliverable: custom response scripts and qualification criteria.
  • Day 7 — Testing: Rigorous testing ensures that the tone and accuracy of responses meet our institutional standards. Deliverable: quality assurance report and final adjustments.
  • Days 7 to 8 — Go Live: A controlled rollout where we monitor every interaction to ensure seamless operation. Deliverable: live infrastructure with real-time monitoring.
  • Ongoing — Improvement: We stay as a partner, fine-tuning the infrastructure as your home reaches peak occupancy. Deliverable: monthly performance reports and optimization.
